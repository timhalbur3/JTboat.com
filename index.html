ABOUT JT BOAT SERVICES

Mobile Boat Detailing Built Around Convenience

JT Boat Services was built to make boat ownership easier. Instead of hauling your boat, waiting at marinas, or dealing with the hassle of scheduling drop-offs, we bring everything directly to you.

We specialize in fully mobile, dockside service across the Madison area — delivering interior detailing, cleaning, and on-lift refueling right where your boat sits. Fast, reliable, and built around your schedule.

Whether you’re preparing for a weekend on the lake or maintaining your boat all season, our goal is simple: keep your boat clean, fueled, and ready — without you lifting a finger.
